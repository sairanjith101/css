Dhanush was born as Venkatesh Prabhu Kasthuri Raja[17] on 28 July 1983 to Tamil film director and producer, Kasthuri Raja, and his wife, Vijayalakshmi, in Madras, Tamil Nadu.[18] Initially he wished to go to a school of hotel management and become a chef.[19] However his brother, director Selvaraghavan, pressured him to become an actor.[20][21] Dhanush also has two sisters named Vimalageetha and Karthiga Karthik.[22]
